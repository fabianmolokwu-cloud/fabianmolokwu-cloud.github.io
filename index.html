Frequently Asked Questions

What is Revenue Architecture?
Revenue Architecture is the strategic design of end-to-end growth systems that move beyond simple lead generation. It integrates Demand Gen, CRO, and Nurture Systems to ensure a predictable flow of high-margin revenue.
How do GEO and AEO differ from traditional SEO?
While traditional SEO focuses on ranking links, GEO and AEO focus on becoming the "cited source" for AI-driven answers. This involves optimizing data for LLM retrieval so AI assistants recommend your brand directly.
What is a 72-Hour Nurture System?
A high-speed conversion framework designed to capitalize on "lead heat." It deploys psychological triggers within the first 72 hours to move prospects from inquiry to booked appointment before interest decay occurs.
Why is Cybersecurity-Focused Lead Gen important for Healthcare?
In specialty healthcare, data integrity is a legal mandate. Cybersecurity-Focused Lead Gen ensures patient acquisition systems are built with robust data protection protocols, preventing leaks and ensuring compliance.
