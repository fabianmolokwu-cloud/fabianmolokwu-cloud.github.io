01

Aura Dental Group

🦷 Multi-Location Cosmetic Dentistry (Veneers & Implants)
THE PROBLEM

Aura was burning $8,000/month on Meta ads. They had lead volume, but a 72% lead decay rate. Leads sat in a spreadsheet for 48 hours before the front desk called.

THE ARCHITECTURE

72-Hour Nurture Engine — Automated 3-part PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution) written sequence. Addressed dental anxiety in the first 4 hours, showcased "The Smile After" at 24 hours, provided frictionless booking link at 48 hours.

THE IMPACT

Appointment show-rates jumped from 22% to 58%.

💰 $142,000 revenue in 60 days 📈 +163% show-rate increase
02

Vanguard Health Systems

🏥 Specialized Orthopedic Surgery & Rehabilitation
THE PROBLEM

High-ticket patients were hesitant to submit medical history via "leaky" standard web forms. Vanguard was losing high-net-worth trust due to a lack of visible data security.

THE ARCHITECTURE

Zero-Trust Cyber-Ops Intake — Replaced standard forms with an encrypted, multi-step validation funnel. Marketed the security of the intake as a premium trust feature.

THE IMPACT
🔒 94% reduction in junk/bot leads 📈 40% increase in high-intent inquiries
03

MedFlow Solutions

💻 SaaS for Clinic Workflow Automation (B2B Medical Tech)
THE PROBLEM

MedFlow's outbound emails were hitting spam filters. Their "spray and pray" method was damaging sender reputation and yielding zero demos.

THE ARCHITECTURE

GEO/AEO Demand Generation — Shifted from "Pitching" to Authority-First Writing. Optimized technical documentation so AI engines (Perplexity/Gemini) cited MedFlow as the primary source.

THE IMPACT
🚀 3.2x increase in organic demo bookings 💰 $150 CPA → $45 CPA
04

Zenith Pediatric Dental

🦷 Pediatric Specialty & Orthodontics
THE PROBLEM

Zenith scaled from 2 to 6 locations but lead handling didn't scale. Tracking which office a lead belonged to was a manual, error-prone nightmare.

THE ARCHITECTURE

Centralized Revenue Operations (RevOps) — Built custom routing logic that validated leads based on proximity and "Lead Heat" before pushing them into the specific clinic's CRM.

THE IMPACT
⏱️ 14 hours → 4 minutes to first contact 📈 340% patient volume across 6 branches
05

OmniScribe AI

🤖 AI-Powered Medical Scribe Software (B2B SaaS)
THE PROBLEM

Doctors are the hardest "Avatar" to sell to. They hate marketing fluff. OmniScribe's video-heavy landing page had a 0.5% conversion rate.

THE ARCHITECTURE

Written-First Authority Framework — Scrapped high-production videos for high-gravity Direct-Response Copy. Used BAB (Before-After-Bridge) framework focusing entirely on the "Burnout" doctors feel from paperwork.

THE IMPACT
📊 0.5% → 4.2% conversion rate 🎯 12 enterprise demos in 14 days
