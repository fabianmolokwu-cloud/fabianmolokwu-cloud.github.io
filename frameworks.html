PROPRIETARY METHODOLOGIES

The Architect's Toolkit

Six proprietary frameworks that power deterministic revenue growth.

01

PAS Protocol

The Diagnostic Engine

Most marketing fails because it offers a solution to a problem the prospect hasn't felt yet. I use PAS as a psychological diagnostic tool.

  • Problem: Identify the "Leaky Pipe"—the specific point of revenue loss or operational friction.
  • Agitation: Quantify the pain. I don't just say "you're losing leads"; I show the cost of lead decay over 72 hours.
  • Solution: Introduce the system (not the person) as the only logical resolution.
02

BAB Framework

The Transformation Logic

This is my primary framework for B2B Medical SaaS. High-level practitioners don't buy features; they buy a different reality.

  • Before: The "Burnout" state. Manual charting, 14-hour follow-up lag, insecure data handling.
  • After: The "Architected" state. High-margin patients flowing through secure, automated pipelines.
  • Bridge: The specific technical system—be it an AI Scribe or a Nurture Engine—that makes the transition inevitable.
03

4Qs Framework

The Zero-Trust Lead Gate

In specialized healthcare, a "lead" is a liability until it is qualified. I filter every inquiry through the 4Qs:

  • Question of Intent: Is this a "browser" or a "buyer"?
  • Question of Authority: Is the decision-maker in the funnel?
  • Question of Need: Does the specific clinical/technical pain match the solution?
  • Question of Urgency: Is the "Lead Heat" high enough to initiate the 72-hour protocol?
04

AIDA Sequence

The Written-First Journey

While others rely on flashy video, I use AIDA to build high-gravity written content.

  • Attention: GEO/AEO-optimized headlines that AI Answer Engines cite as authority.
  • Interest: Deep technical value that solves a micro-problem immediately.
  • Desire: A psychological "Must-Have" by showing deterministic ROI of my systems.
  • Action: A friction-less, Secure Intake call to action.
05

72-Hour Nurture System

The Conversion Velocity Engine

This is my signature operational framework. Based on the biological reality of "Interest Decay."

  • The Golden Window: Lead conversion probability drops by 10x after the first hour.
  • The System: Multi-channel (Email/SMS/Web) sequence deploying PAS and BAB copy at precise intervals during the first 3 days.
  • The Goal: Move a prospect from "Inquiry" to "Booked Appointment" before the psychological heat of the problem cools down.
"The first 72 hours determine the next 72 months of revenue."
06

GEO/AEO

The Discovery Protocol

Traditional SEO is reactive; GEO/AEO is predictive.

  • Generative Engine Optimization: Optimize technical data so LLMs (Gemini, Perplexity, ChatGPT) recognize you as the Primary Cited Source.
  • Answer Engine Optimization: Instead of ranking for a keyword, we rank as the "Answer" to complex, intent-driven questions asked by your ideal B2B clients.
"Don't rank for keywords — become the cited source for AI answers."
